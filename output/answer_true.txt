At the 2022 Winter Olympics, the gold medal in curling was won by the following teams:

- **Men's Curling**: The Swedish team, skipped by Niklas Edin, won the gold medal.
- **Women's Curling**: The Great Britain team, skipped by Eve Muirhead, won the gold medal.
- **Mixed Doubles Curling**: The Italian team, consisting of Stefania Constantini and Amos Mosaner, won the gold medal.
