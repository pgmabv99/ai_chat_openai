At the 2022 Winter Olympics, the gold medals in curling were won by the following teams:

- **Men's Curling**: The Swedish team, skipped by Niklas Edin, won the gold medal. This was a historic win for the team, marking Sweden's first Olympic gold in men's curling.
  
- **Women's Curling**: The Great Britain team, skipped by Eve Muirhead, won the gold medal. This victory marked Great Britain's first Olympic gold in women's curling since the sport was officially added to the Olympic program in 1998.

- **Mixed Doubles Curling**: The Italian team, consisting of Stefania Constantini and Amos Mosaner, won the gold medal. This was Italy's first Olympic medal in curling.
